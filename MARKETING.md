## App Description (Full)

**Take control of your finances with DriftMoney — the intelligent personal finance app that makes money management effortless.**

DriftMoney combines powerful budgeting tools with AI-driven insights to help you understand your spending, track your bills, and reach your savings goals faster. Whether you're managing multiple bank accounts, paying down credit cards, or saving for something special, DriftMoney gives you the complete picture of your financial life in one beautiful app.

### Why DriftMoney?

**All Your Accounts in One Place**
Connect and track bank accounts, credit cards, and loans. See your complete net worth at a glance and understand exactly where you stand financially.

**Smart Bill Tracking**
Never miss a payment again. Track recurring bills, get reminders before due dates, and see exactly how much you need to set aside for upcoming expenses.

**AI-Powered Insights**
Our intelligent features analyze your spending patterns to detect unusual transactions, suggest realistic budgets, and forecast your cash flow weeks in advance.

**Beautiful Analytics**
Visualize your spending by category, track trends over time, and compare month-to-month to see your progress. Know exactly where your money goes.

**Goal-Oriented Savings**
Set savings goals with target dates, track your progress, and stay motivated with clear visualizations of how close you are to achieving your dreams.

**Privacy First**
Your financial data stays on your device. No selling your data to third parties. Optional cloud sync keeps your data backed up and available across devices.

## Press Kit Summary

**What is DriftMoney?**
DriftMoney is a comprehensive personal finance app that combines traditional budgeting tools with AI-powered insights. It helps users track all their accounts, manage bills, set budgets, save toward goals, and understand their spending — all while keeping their data private and secure.

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **AI Integration** — Uses local LLM technology to provide smart categorization, anomaly detection, budget recommendations, and cash flow forecasting
2. **Complete Account Support** — Tracks bank accounts, credit cards, and loans with type-specific features
3. **Privacy-First** — Local-first architecture means your data stays on your device
4. **OCR Import** — Scan statements to automatically import transactions
5. **Comprehensive Analytics** — From simple category breakdowns to complex trend analysis

**Target Audience:**
- Young professionals managing finances independently
- Families tracking household budgets
- Anyone paying down debt (credit cards, loans)
- Goal-oriented savers
- Privacy-conscious users who want control of their data
